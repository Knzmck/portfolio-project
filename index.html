about me

My background and education are in Healthcare as a Registered Nurse. I attended the University of Kansas from 2013-2016 and Saint Luke's College of Health Sciences from 2016-2017 graduating with a Bachelor's degree in Nursing Sciences. In college, I worked as a CNA in a medical telemetry unit at the University of Kansas Hospital. After graduation, I worked as a float nurse between 14 different units at Saint Luke's Hospital in Kansas City. I am currently a marketing consultant and partial owner of a nutraceutical company called HGSD. In my free time I volunteer for the League of Women's Voters in Johnson County and assist with their social media marketing. I am enrolled in a technical program at KU to learn website development and coding.
